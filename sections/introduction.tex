\frametitle{INTRODUCTION}
\begin{itemize}
			\item Malnutrition is defined as imbalance, deficiencies, or excess nutrients intake. It is classified as undernutrition or overnutrition
			\item Undernutrition is either \textcolor {blue} {stunting} (low height for age), \textcolor {blue} {underweight} (low weight for age), and \textcolor {blue} {wasting} (low weight for height) ( Kenya: Nutrition Profile, 2021).
			\item Proper nutrition is a crucial factor for the growth a development of children under five.
		\begin {block} {PROBLEM STATEMENT}
			\item In Kenya, malnutrition rates are a cause for concern, with high numbers in certain regions. It exposes children to various infections, which complicate the outcome 
			\item Results from the Kenya Demographic Health Survey (2014/2015) indicate that approximately {26\alert\%} of the children under five are stunted, \alert {4\%} are wasted, and \alert {11\%} are underweight. 
		\end {block} \part{title}
		\end{itemize}
