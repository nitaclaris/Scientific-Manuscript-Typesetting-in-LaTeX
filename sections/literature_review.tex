		\begin{itemize}
			\item Several policies and measures have been put in place in the country to address the issue of malnutrition. \textcolor {blue} {Although these policies have helped counter the rising rate of malnutrition, cases are still persistent.} 
			\item Socioeconomic factors associate social and economic factors. They are lifestyle elements and measurements of both social standing and financial viability. These include: \textcolor {blue} {maternal nutrition, maternal education, wealth index} and \textcolor {blue} {place of residence.}
			\item Bio-demographic factors are factors in which biological considerations such as evolutionary, epidemiological and genetic determinants are accentuated. Child health and survival can be affected by alterations in reproductive patterns through various mechanisms, more so through changes in; \textcolor {blue} {birth order, maternal age at birth or birth intervals} (Sathiya Susuman et al., 2016).
