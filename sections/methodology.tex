\begin{frame}
\frametitle{METHODOLOGY}
\begin{itemize}
	\item The study will use the Kenya Demographic Health Survey (KDHS 2014) dataset. Specifically, the interest is with the Children’s recode (KEKR72FL) and Women’s recode (KEIR72FL).
	\item Descriptive statistics will be done to summarize the data quantitatively.
	\item The summary statistics will also give a general outlook of the data. 
	\item Bivariable analysis will be conducted to examine the strengths of the relationship among variables of interest.
	\item For multivariable analysis, the model applied will be the Modified Poisson Regression model. 
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
\begin{frame}
\frametitle{BIVARIABLE ANALYSIS}
\begin{itemize}
	\item This analysis provides percentage distributions of malnutrition status by each of the independent variable. 
	\item Chi- square test will be used to determine the strength of the relationship between the dependent and each independent variable to establish whether the association is statistically significant or not. 
	\item Precisely, the test will determine the degree of association between malnutrition status and various independent variables among children under five. 
	
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
